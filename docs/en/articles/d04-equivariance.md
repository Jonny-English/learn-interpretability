## Summary

Find transformed filter pairs live, compare rotated and translated responses, and inspect how feature families move together.

## Notebook Scope

The notebook demonstrates equivariant structure by searching weight-space similarities and then validating them with stimulus sweeps.

## Live Coverage

- Rotation-similar filter-pair search in Inception branches
- Rotated arc response sweeps for matched channels
- Translated-stimulus response consistency checks

## Integrity Note

This is a teaching-scale live analysis of equivariant structure in public InceptionV1 weights and activations, not a claim to reproduce every Distill case study.
