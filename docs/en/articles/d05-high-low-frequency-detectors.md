## Summary

Generate directional high-low frequency transitions live and measure which channels prefer these frequency edges.

## Notebook Scope

The notebook synthesizes frequency-edge stimuli at runtime and searches for channels that respond strongly and selectively to them.

## Live Coverage

- High-low frequency stimulus generation
- Channel search for frequency-edge preference
- Orientation tuning for the best frequency detector

## Integrity Note

The stimuli are generated live with simple public code. The resulting figures are runtime measurements, not bundled replicas of the Distill artwork.
